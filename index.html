Hamster, The Candy Hunter
→ : Go right
↓ : Go down
ENTER : Restart
How to play :
  1. Game starts with 3 tokens.
  2. There are only 2 moves, right and down.
  3. Press → of the keyboard to go right. Press ↓ to go down.
  4. Hold the key to move and release to stop.
  5. Landing on a candy grants extra turn.
  6. 1 turn is worth 1 token.
  7. Game ends when tokens reach 0.
